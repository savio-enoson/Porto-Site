Car-centric infrastructure is often the norm in urban areas. Consequently, a common frustration among residents is the difficulty and inefficiency in finding parking spaces, especially in more crowded establishments such as shopping malls and universities.

Parkibara aims to provide accurate and reliable information on availability while streamlining the process overall, mainly through providing a user-centric platform with various intuitive features. For venues with paid parking, Parkibara works with a split-revenue model, whereas for venues with free parking such as educational institutions a service subscription model will be available.

Features

Real-Time Data

Users can view parking statistics real-time before departing. Venues can also be marked as favorites for easier access. This information is updated real-time using each venue's database.

Integrated Payment System

Direct integration with various electronic wallets to streamline the payment process. Payment can be done either through the app or through traditional methods (cash or debit card upon exit).

Granular Information

Distributed systems allow for more detailed information to be served with minimal strain on the main servers. Thus, granular information down to each parking slot's occupancy can be tracked.

Parking Tracker

When users are parked, Parkibara will remember the spot the user has taken, along with guides to get to the specific sector, in case they get lost or forget where they parked.

Electronic Parking Ticket

Receipts are stored digitally to reduce paper usage. This also eliminates security fees usually charged when visitors lose their parking tickets. Physical tickets will still be available for visitors without the app.

SOS Services

Emergency services can be contacted through the app. Visitors can also establish contact with the venue security services directly, eliminating the need to look for personnel during emergencies.

Parking Activity

Activity / usage history is tracked through the app. Also tracks the specific vehicle used for each session, as well as additional information such as duration and parking fees.
